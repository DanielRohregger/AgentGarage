What good preparation looks like

  • The use case is specific and easy to explain.
  • Relevant systems and usable data are already known.
  • Expected outputs and success criteria are measurable.
  • Access, permissions, and key risks are visible early.

Important note

Agent Garage outcome: a validated prototype that proves usefulness and feasibility. The production version usually requires further refinement, governance checks, testing, and expansion.
